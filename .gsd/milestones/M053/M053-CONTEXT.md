# M053: App-Wide GSD Workbench Redesign

The GSD planning experience should not feel isolated inside Plan Builder. The full desktop app needs the same workbench language: source-proof surfaces, workflow phase cues, dense operational panels, and durable planning terminology across sessions, settings, skills, extensions, diffs, and the composer.

## Success Criteria

- The redesign pulls directly from the reference images: framed desktop workbench, dark split panes, bottom composer, right rail, source-proof windows, and restrained purple/blue ambient frame.
- The app shell communicates GSD as the primary workflow from first launch through settings and review.
- The redesign preserves existing navigation names and app behavior while adding GSD terminology around them.
- Dark and light modes share the same design system without washed-out or low-contrast panels.
- Follow-up slices can continue view-by-view without reworking the global styling foundation.
