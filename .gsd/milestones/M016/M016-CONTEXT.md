# M016: Composer-Driven Change Proposal Review

## Vision

After a parked composer idea becomes a change draft, the composer should keep guiding the user to the existing proposal review surface. The goal is not to approve changes from chat, but to remove the last bit of hunting between "drafted" and the concrete injection or modification forms.

## Success Criteria

- Composer-parked ideas with draft proposals expose a review handoff.
- The handoff focuses the existing change proposal card and approval form.
- Injection and modification approval still use the existing side-pane forms.
- Electron coverage proves the visible handoff works without changing workflow phase.

## Non-Goals

- Approving injections or modifications directly from the composer.
- Replacing existing change proposal cards.
- Changing projection or event semantics.
