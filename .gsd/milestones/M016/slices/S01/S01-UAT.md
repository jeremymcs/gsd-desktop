# S01 UAT: Composer Reviews Draft Proposal

A user parks a later-phase note, prepares it, drafts a change, saves the draft, then clicks Review proposal from the composer follow-up.

## Expected

- Review proposal appears after the draft is saved.
- Clicking it focuses the existing change proposal approval form.
- The proposal remains editable through the side-pane form.
- The active workflow phase is unchanged.
