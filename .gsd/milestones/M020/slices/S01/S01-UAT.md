# S01 UAT: Correct Draft Proposal Details

A user parks an idea, prepares it, creates a draft change proposal, notices the draft details need refinement, edits the draft, and keeps reviewing the revised proposal.

## Expected

- The draft proposal exposes `Edit draft details`.
- Saving revised title, summary, and impact notes updates the proposal card.
- Injection and modification editors are not shown while details are being edited.
- After restart, the revised draft proposal details are still visible.
