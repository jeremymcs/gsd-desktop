# S01 UAT: Idea Pool Reviews Draft Proposal

A user parks an idea, prepares it, saves a draft change proposal, then uses the idea pool to review the proposal.

## Expected

- The idea pool item shows `Review proposal` after the draft is saved.
- The disabled `Drafted` action is absent.
- Clicking `Review proposal` focuses the existing change proposal approval form.
- The composer review action still works.
