# S02 UAT: Explicit Research Override

## Scenario

1. Create a plan.
2. Save `not sure` for the project name.
3. Complete and confirm all DISCUSS depth gates.
4. Inspect the DISCUSS complete handoff.
5. Check the readiness acknowledgement.

- The readiness warning calls out high-signal unresolved guidance.
- `Start research` is disabled before acknowledgement.
- The acknowledgement says `Continue to RESEARCH with unresolved high-signal guidance`.
