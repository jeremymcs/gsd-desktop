# S03: PLAN Readiness Warning

## Goal

Unresolved DISCUSS guidance should remain visible after RESEARCH is accepted and before users start PLAN.

## Scope

- Reuse the readiness warning on the RESEARCH approved handoff.
- Keep `Start plan` available in this slice.
- Extend Electron coverage through accepted research to the PLAN handoff.
- Keep readiness warning derived from saved answers only.

## Acceptance

- A plan with unresolved DISCUSS guidance shows the warning on the Ready for PLAN card.
- The warning includes the unresolved count.
- `Start plan` remains enabled.
- Suggested follow-up prompts are still not persisted as answers.
