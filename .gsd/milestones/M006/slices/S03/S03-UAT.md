# S03 UAT: Requirement-Aware Guidance

## Scenario

1. Create a plan in Plan Builder.
2. Complete and confirm the Project discussion stage.
3. In Requirements, answer the capabilities question with `yes`.
4. Save the answer.

## Expected Result

- Discussion memory shows an adaptive follow-up on the Capabilities answer.
- The follow-up is a medium signal.
- The signal chips include `R001`, `functional requirement`, and `unvalidated`.
- The rationale states that the answer feeds `R001` before it steers PLAN.
- The requirements contract renders the corresponding `R001` row from existing planning state.
