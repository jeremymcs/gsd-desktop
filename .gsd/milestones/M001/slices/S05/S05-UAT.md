# S05 UAT: Research Staging and Approval

## Scenario

1. Open a workspace in the desktop app.
2. Open Plans.
3. Create a plan.
4. Complete DISCUSS project, requirements, and milestone questions.
5. Confirm each depth gate.
6. Click **Start research**.
7. Edit the research title and findings.
8. Click **Stage research**.
9. Accept the proposed research output.
10. Restart the app and reopen Plans.

## Expected Result

- The RESEARCH phase becomes active only after DISCUSS is confirmed.
- Proposed research appears in the pending review list.
- Accepted research moves to accepted research history.
- After restart, the plan, discussion memory, and accepted research output are restored from `.gsd/gsd.db`.
