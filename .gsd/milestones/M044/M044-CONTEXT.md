# M044: Guardrail Stop/Resume UX

Guardrail warnings are now visible, but users need clearer stop and resume behavior when a warning blocks autonomous work. This milestone turns warnings into an operational stop panel with direct recovery actions.

## Success Criteria

- Blocking warnings are separated from informational warnings.
- Users can jump to the recovery target or repair action from the warning surface.
- The UI explains whether autonomous work is stopped, resumable, or safe.
