# S01: Stop Surface Actions

**Goal:** Promote guardrail warnings into actionable stop/resume UI.

## Tasks

- [ ] Classify guardrail warnings as blocking or informational.
- [ ] Add recovery or repair actions where the app has deterministic actions.
- [ ] Keep normal queue inspection available while autopilot is blocked.
- [ ] Cover restart and action behavior in Electron tests.

## Acceptance

- A user can understand why autopilot stopped and choose the next deterministic recovery action.
